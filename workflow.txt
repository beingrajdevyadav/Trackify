Sure! Here's a typical workflow for managing a task from assignment to completion:

1. **Task Assigned:** The task is given to an individual or team.
2. **Task Accepted:** The person acknowledges and accepts the task.
3. **Planning:** The person plans the steps and resources needed to complete the task.
4. **In Progress:** The task is actively being worked on.
5. **Pending/On Hold:** The task is temporarily paused, awaiting further information or resources.
6. **Review:** The task is completed and is under review by the relevant stakeholders.
7. **Completed:** The task is successfully completed and approved.
8. **Archived:** The task is documented and stored for future reference.
